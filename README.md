## 🦆 DUCK HUNT — play right here!

A duck is flying across my profile **right now**. Think you can shoot it?

**How to play**

1. The duck flies a fixed loop, synced to the **UTC clock** — each sky zone shows the seconds window when the duck is there (e.g. `:00 - :07`).
2. Watch the seconds on your clock, pick the zone the duck will be in **when you click Create**, and hit a 🎯 target below.
3. That opens a pre-filled issue — just press **Create**. A robot referee scores your shot in ~30s, comments HIT or MISS, and updates the leaderboard.
4. Shots one zone off still count (you winged it). One shot per hunter every 30 seconds.
